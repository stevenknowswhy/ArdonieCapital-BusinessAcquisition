Express Deal Success Stories

By David Rodriguez
• January 5, 2024 • 12 min read

Our Express Deal program has helped dozens of auto shop owners complete successful transactions in just 34 days. Here are three inspiring stories that showcase the power of our streamlined process.

🚖 Express Deal Program

Our Express Deal program connects pre-qualified buyers with motivated sellers, completing transactions in 34 days or less with full support throughout the process.

Story 1: From Retirement Dream to Reality

Tom Martinez

Seller - Martinez Auto Service, Plano, TX

"After 28 years of running my shop, I was ready to retire but worried about finding the right buyer. The Express Deal program not only found me a qualified buyer but completed the entire transaction in just 31 days."

Tom Martinez had built Martinez Auto Service into a thriving business over nearly three decades. With annual revenues of $1.2M and a loyal customer base, Tom knew his shop was valuable – but he also knew that finding the right buyer could take months or even years.

The Challenge

  • Tom wanted to retire by his 65th birthday, giving him a tight timeline
  • He needed to find a buyer who would maintain his shop's reputation and retain his employees
  • Traditional business brokers quoted 6-12 month timelines
  • Tom was concerned about maintaining business operations during a lengthy sale process

The Express Deal Solution

Within 48 hours of Tom's initial inquiry, our team had:

  • Completed a preliminary valuation of $875K based on financial performance
  • Identified three pre-qualified buyers from our network
  • Scheduled initial meetings with interested parties
  • Begun the due diligence process with our streamlined documentation system

The Outcome

Tom accepted an offer of $850K from Maria Gonzalez, an experienced technician looking to become an owner. The transaction closed in 31 days, and Tom was able to retire on schedule. Six months later, the shop continues to thrive under Maria's ownership with all original employees retained.

Story 2: First-Time Buyer's Dream Come True

Maria Gonzalez

Buyer - Now Owner of Martinez Auto Service

"I had been saving for years to buy my own shop but didn't know where to start. The Express Deal program guided me through every step and helped me secure SBA financing. Now I'm living my dream as a business owner."

Maria Gonzalez had worked as a master technician for 12 years, specializing in European vehicles. She had always dreamed of owning her own shop but found the process of finding and financing a business overwhelming.

The Challenge

  • Limited experience with business acquisitions and financing
  • Needed guidance on valuation and due diligence
  • Required SBA loan approval within tight timeframes
  • Wanted to find a shop with growth potential and good reputation

The Express Deal Advantage

Our team provided comprehensive support:

  • Connected Maria with SBA-preferred lenders in our network
  • Provided detailed financial analysis and valuation guidance
  • Facilitated meetings with Tom to ensure cultural fit
  • Coordinated due diligence with legal and accounting professionals
  • Managed timeline to ensure all parties met their deadlines

The Result

Maria secured SBA financing for 80% of the purchase price and closed on her dream business. In her first six months of ownership, she's increased revenue by 15% through expanded European vehicle services and improved customer retention programs.

Story 3: Multi-Location Expansion Success

James Wilson

Buyer - Wilson Automotive Group

"We were looking to expand into the North Dallas market but needed to move quickly on the right opportunity. Express Deal helped us identify and acquire two complementary shops in just 28 days."

James Wilson already owned two successful auto repair shops in Fort Worth and was looking to expand into North Dallas. He needed to find shops that would complement his existing operations and could be integrated quickly.

The Opportunity

Our team identified two shops in Plano and Richardson that were perfect fits:

  • Both shops had strong financial performance but aging owners
  • Complementary service offerings (one specialized in transmissions, the other in general repair)
  • Established customer bases with minimal overlap
  • Opportunities for operational synergies and cost savings

The Express Deal Process

We coordinated a complex dual acquisition:

  • Structured the deals to close simultaneously
  • Negotiated favorable terms for both acquisitions
  • Coordinated due diligence across multiple properties
  • Facilitated financing for the combined $1.4M purchase price
  • Managed integration planning to ensure smooth transitions

The Impact

James successfully expanded his operation and achieved significant synergies. The combined revenue of all four locations increased by 22% in the first year through cross-referrals, shared marketing, and operational efficiencies.
