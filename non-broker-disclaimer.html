What We Are

Ardonie Capital operates BuyMartV1 as a marketplace platform that facilitates connections between buyers and sellers of auto repair businesses. Our services include:

  • Providing a digital marketplace for listing and discovering auto repair businesses for sale
  • Offering educational resources and informational content about business acquisitions
  • Facilitating communication between potential buyers and sellers
  • Providing tools and calculators for informational purposes
  • Connecting users with qualified professionals and service providers

What We Are NOT

We explicitly DO NOT provide the following services:

  • Investment advice or recommendations - We do not advise on whether to buy, sell, or hold any business or investment
  • Broker-dealer services - We do not execute transactions or act as intermediaries in securities transactions
  • Financial planning or advisory services - We do not provide personalized financial advice
  • Valuation services - Our tools provide estimates for informational purposes only
  • Legal advice - We do not provide legal counsel or representation
  • Fiduciary services - We do not act as fiduciaries for our users

Seek Professional Advice

We strongly recommend that you consult with qualified, licensed professionals before making any business acquisition decisions. This includes, but is not limited to:

Financial Professionals:

  • Licensed business brokers
  • Certified Public Accountants (CPAs)
  • Financial advisors
  • Business valuation experts

Legal & Other Professionals:

  • Business attorneys
  • Tax professionals
  • Insurance agents
  • Industry consultants

Risk Disclosures

Business acquisitions involve significant risks and potential for loss. Important considerations include:

  • Financial Risk: You may lose some or all of your investment
  • Market Risk: Business values and market conditions can change
  • Operational Risk: Businesses may not perform as expected
  • Liquidity Risk: Business investments may be difficult to sell quickly
  • Due Diligence: Thorough investigation is essential before any purchase
  • Legal Compliance: Ensure all transactions comply with applicable laws
