Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How quickly can I expect a response?

We respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. Express Path inquiries typically receive a response within 4-6 hours.

What is the Express Path program?

Express Path is our 34-day transaction program for qualified buyers and sellers. It includes priority matching, dedicated support, and a guaranteed timeline from initial contact to closing.

Do you charge fees for initial consultations?

No, initial consultations and platform access are completely free. We only earn when you successfully complete a transaction through our marketplace.

What areas do you serve?

We focus exclusively on the Dallas-Fort Worth metropolitan area, including Dallas, Fort Worth, Plano, Arlington, Irving, Garland, and surrounding communities.

Can you help with financing?

Yes, our network includes SBA lenders and alternative financing specialists who understand auto repair shop acquisitions. We can connect you with pre-approved financing options.

What if I'm not ready to buy or sell immediately?

That's perfectly fine! We can help you prepare for a future transaction, provide market insights, and keep you informed about opportunities that match your criteria.
