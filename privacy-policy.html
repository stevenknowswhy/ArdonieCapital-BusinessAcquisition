Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information

Last updated: July 1, 2025

🔒 Our Privacy Commitment

Ardonie Capital ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and maintaining the confidentiality of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our auto repair shop marketplace and Express Deal services.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, and mailing address
  • Business Information: Company details, auto shop listings, and business credentials
  • Financial Information: Payment details and transaction history
  • Professional Credentials: Licenses, certifications, and qualifications
  • Account Information: Username, password, and profile preferences

Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect certain information when you visit our website:

  • IP address and browser information
  • Device information and operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on our site
  • Referring website information and search terms
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

How We Use Your Information

📊 Data Usage Purposes

Service Provision: Provide and maintain our marketplace platform and Express Deal services

Transaction Processing: Process payments, transactions, and business communications

Communication: Send service updates, notifications, and marketing communications (with consent)

Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to improve our website and user experience

Legal Compliance: Comply with legal obligations and regulatory requirements

Security: Prevent fraud, ensure security, and protect our users and platform

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize us to share your information
  • Transaction Completion: With other parties to complete transactions you request
  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party service providers who assist our operations
  • Legal Requirements: To comply with legal obligations, court orders, or government requests
  • Safety and Rights: To protect our rights, safety, and the rights of our users
  • Business Transfers: In connection with mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales

Data Security

🔐 Security Measures

Technical Safeguards: We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Organizational Measures: Our team follows strict data handling procedures and access controls.

Encryption: Sensitive data is encrypted both in transit and at rest.

Regular Audits: We regularly review and update our security practices.

Your Privacy Rights

✅ Your Rights Include

Access: Request access to your personal information we hold

Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information

Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal requirements)

Objection: Object to processing of your information for certain purposes

Portability: Request transfer of your data to another service provider

Withdrawal: Withdraw consent for processing where consent is the legal basis

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our website. For detailed information about our cookie practices, please see our Cookie Policy.

Third-Party Services

🔗 Service Providers & External Links

Our platform integrates with various third-party services to provide enhanced functionality. Each service has its own privacy policy and data handling practices:

Google Services: We use Google Analytics and other Google services for website analytics, performance monitoring, and enhanced functionality. Google Privacy Policy

Supabase: Our database and backend infrastructure are powered by Supabase, which processes and stores your data securely. Supabase Privacy Policy

Clerk.com: User authentication, account management, and identity verification services are provided by Clerk. Clerk Privacy Policy

Stripe: Payment processing, billing, and financial transaction services are handled by Stripe. Stripe Privacy Policy

Data Sharing: When you use our services, certain information may be shared with these providers as necessary to deliver the requested functionality. We encourage you to review their privacy policies to understand how your data is handled.

External Links: Our website may also contain links to other third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites and encourage you to read their privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

Changes to This Policy

📝 Policy Updates

Update Rights: We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements.

Notification: We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Review Responsibility: We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
